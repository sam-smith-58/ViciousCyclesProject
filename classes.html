Love motorcycles, but don't know how to drive one?

We've got you.

We have classes that teach beginner bikers (it could be you!) how to ride. Through our lessons, not only will we show you how to be safe on the road, we'll show you how to have fun too.

Lessons take place on scheduled days:

  • Mon-Wed: beginner riding lessons 5 p.m.-7 p.m.
  • Thurs: how to repair you ride workshop 8 p.m.- 10 p.m.
  • Fri: riding in extreme weather training 4 p.m.- 5 p.m.
  • Sun: how to ride with passengers (safely) 12 p.m.- 2 p.m.
